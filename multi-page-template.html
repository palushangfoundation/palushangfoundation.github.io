ABOUT US

Palushang foundation was founded in 2012 by Patron Palushang. We are a group of remarkable guitar making enthusiasts with the main goal to shape, encourage, and share life skills with the upcoming generation. One of many of our missions is to increase technological knowledge to the community by teaching basic computer skills. We also aim to eliminate idleness, joblessness, domestic abuse, lack of education and famine.
Our focus is on the orphans and widowed, we believe that everyone deserves a healthy and beneficial lifestyle. We strive to spread as much positivity and wellness as possible, knowing that we had nothing and had to struggle for basic life needs, we want to help those who are there now. Our community is very supportive in what we do and wish we continue this mission.
Our belief: We believe that by sharing our knowledge and talents is the most important gift to offer to the world.  

Palushang Patron

Manager Director

Our team

Patron Palushang is the general manager and the founder of Palushang Music Company. As the main vision carrier for the foundation, Patron has worked hard over the years to provide great and sufficient services to the community and made sure his peers are well supported. In 1998 Patron acquired the skills necessary to construct a guitar from the beginning stages and/or foundation, to now selling over 10 countries.
About 20 years later, Palushang Foundation launched in Australia with more than 20 community members on board.
Patron’s goal is to teach people new and beneficial skills whilst helping them overcome what life may bring.
Patron aims to eliminate idleness, domestic abuse, famine, and many more community issues. In doing this, he aims to teach them how to play and make guitars from scratch. He believes that knowing these skills will increase one’s self-confidence, identity and most importantly, mental health.
He also aims to promote new artists in the community and to allow them to find a pathway suitable for their talent. 

Patron Palushang
General manager

Faraja is a talented and outgoing educator who joined our team in 2012. Before joining, Faraja was making and selling her necklaces to the local markets and teaching how to make them. Her vision was to help unemployed women in the refugee camp, she did this by starting her necklace making mentor group. She is a strong believer in equality and fairness. After hearing about us in 2012, she immediately joined the team and has ever since been a key member. We loved Faraja’s vision to eliminate the early marriages in the camp and the raping of young women by providing jobs and life skills. Her role in team is no different to her vision as she is still making necklaces and helping local women in the community

Faraja Palushang
Talended Educator 


Mugomoka is an exceptional asset to the team. He is a guitar builder in Congo based in Bukavu. He built his first guitar in 1977 and supplied to all the local churches. He taught Patron the fundamentals of guitar building and sound acoustics. Now in 2019, he is a proud mentor and advisor for the foundation.
