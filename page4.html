We are a group of remarkable Guitar making enthusiasts with a goal to shape and encourage young guitar makers. We believe with great skills comes great responsibility to spread what we know to people like us.
We have been making guitars since 1998, and with the skills, knowledge and experience we have obtained, we see it is appropriate to teach and spread our knowledge to young creative minds. We mainly believe that our young creators in our communities need a platform to showcase their creativity, and this is a great way to do that.
With your help we will be doing the community a great favor. Being a non-for profit organisation we strive to help out and be there for the generation to come after us.
