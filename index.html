PALUSHANG FOUNDATION

Founded in 2012 by Patron Palushang, Palushang foundation is a group of outstanding guitar making enthusiasts with the main goal to shape, encourage and share life skills with the upcoming generation. We have utilized our long years of experience in marketing, woodwork and sound acoustics to create exceptional industry standard acoustic and electric guitars. We believe that by sharing our knowledge and talents, is the best gift to offer to the world.

Our promise is to bring your dream guitar to life, hence our moto “We give new life to dead woods”. With every guitar sold, half of the profit goes directly and automatically towards the orphans, widowed and unemployed. With your support, we can make a difference in someone’s life.

 The brand new PalushangCaster

Our brand new PalushangCaster was introduced to the world and on the big stage on the 21/09/2019 in Brisbane Australia. The results were very satisfying and captured the attention of many enthusiasts. 

 The Brown Sunburst Strat neck, 6 string has an exception tone, with: 

• Dimarzio
• true valvet neck gold,
• Toneride Alnico 2
• bridge pickup,
• Queensland maple body,
• Nitro cellulose finish,
• American cherry neck,
• Rock maple fretboard,
• Gotoh hard tail bridge,
• Graph tech tusq nut,  
• two very accurate volume and tone control knobs and gold finish tuning knobs. Now for sale and ready to ship. Grab yours today. Remember, your purchase also goes towards the orphans in Eastern Congo affected by the Ebola virus.
